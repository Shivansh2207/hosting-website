Our Story

Maulik Forwarders Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted and experienced name in the field of Clearing, Forwarding, and Shipping, serving clients across India for over 30 years. Established with the vision of providing seamless logistics and compliance solutions, we specialize in handling all types of documentation and customs clearance related to Export-Import (EXIM) business.

Over the years, we have successfully managed and streamlined operations across India's major seaports, airports, and inland container depots (ICDs). Our expertise spans key hubs such as Nhava Sheva (JNPT), Mumbai Port Trust (BPT), Mundra, Pipavav, Kandla, Chennai, and Kolkata. We also operate efficiently at critical air cargo terminals like Mumbai Air Cargo and Delhi Air Cargo, along with strategic inland points like Delhi ICD and Ahmedabad. With a deep understanding of port regulations, customs procedures, and local logistics, we ensure smooth, reliable, and timely clearance of consignments across the country’s most important trade gateways.

In addition to forwarding and clearance, Maulik Forwarders also offers professional assistance in obtaining Import/Export Code Numbers (IEC) from the office of the Joint Director General of Foreign Trade (DGFT). We help businesses with registrations under various export promotion councils and coordinate with both government and non-government bodies to secure essential documents like Certificates of Origin and other regulatory approvals.

We are committed to offering the most competitive shipping rates, ensuring value for our clients without compromising on speed or service. Our goal is to simplify international trade by delivering end-to-end support with transparency and integrity.
