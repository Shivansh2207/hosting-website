Export & Import Documentation

Complete guide to required documentation for international trade. Access all necessary forms and requirements for seamless customs clearance.

Export Documents
  • Basic Requirements:
    • Invoices (9 Copies)
    • Packing List (9 Copies)
    • Letter of Credit/Order/Contract
  • Mandatory Documents:
    • Export Declarations
    • GR Form (duplicate)
    • Income Tax Number
    • Importer/Exporter Code
  • Additional Documents:
    • Forwarding Instructions
    • E.I.A. Certificate

Additional Export Documentation:

  • Certificate of Origin
  • Quality Control Certificates
  • Pre-shipment Inspection Certificate
  • GST Related Documents
  • Bank Realization Certificate

Special Export Requirements:

  • Export Promotion Copy
  • Shipping Bill (5 copies)
  • ARE-1 Form (if applicable)
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Letter of Credit Compliance Documents

For assistance with export documentation, please contact our office

DEEC/DEPB Documents
  • Primary Documents:
    • Pro forma Invoice (2 copies)
    • DEEC/DEPB Book
    • Application with Receipt
  • Supporting Documents:
    • Declarations
    • Drawback Claims
    • Bank Details
    • Manufacturer Details

DEEC Specific Requirements:

  • Input-Output Norms
  • Export Obligation Documents
  • Previous License Details
  • Raw Material Import Details

DEPB Specific Requirements:

  • Value Addition Details
  • DEPB Rate Sheet
  • Previous DEPB Scrips
  • Port of Registration Details

Need help with DEEC/DEPB? Our experts are here to guide you

Import Documents
  • Import/Export Code (DGFT)
  • Permanent Income Tax Account Number
  • Commercial Documents:
    • Signed Invoice & Packing List
    • Bill of Lading/Airway Bill
    • Insurance Policy/Memo
  • Import License/OGL Declaration
  • GATT & Importer's Declarations
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Bank Documents:
    • Letter of Credit/Bank Draft
    • Bank Endorsed Documents
    • Freight Certificate (if applicable)
  • Special Requirements:
    • DEEC/DEPB Documents
    • N-Form and Undertaking (Mumbai)
    • Duty Bonds (for Warehousing)

Additional Documentation Details:

  • Literature/Certificate of Analysis/Catalogue
  • No Commission Declaration
  • Packing Material Declaration (PN.99/90)
  • Concessional Duty Certificates
  • Ad-hoc Exemption Certificates

For Bonded Warehouse (Outside Mumbai):

  • Double-Duty Bond
  • Transit Bond
  • Space Availability Certificate
  • NOC from Customs/Central Excise
  • Transit Insurance Policy

For comprehensive import documentation support, contact our team

Mumbai Export Requirements
  • EDI Number
  • N-Form with Undertaking
  • Phytosanitary Certificates
  • SGS Certification
  • Fumigation Certificates
  • Palletisation Details
